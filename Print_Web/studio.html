Our Pillars

What sets us apart

Four commitments that define every piece we create.

01 🔬

Microscopic Precision

Every engraving, every fabric texture, every armor clasp — sculpted with a resolution that defies the naked eye. Our miniatures withstand the most demanding macro photography.

  • Surface resolution < 28 microns
  • Individualized facial details
  • Authentic organic textures
02 🏛️

Narrative Sculpting

Our artists do not just model — they tell stories. Every pose, every glance, every accessory is designed to embody the character's story, not just their form.

  • Specialized narrative artists
  • Historical and mythological references
  • Coherent artistic direction
03 ✨

Collector Finish

A micro-bead sanded surface, smoothed, with no visible layer lines. Ready for painting or magnificent as is — our finish is a collector industry standard.

  • Painter-ready smooth surface
  • Studio painting option available
  • Piece-by-piece quality inspection
04 💬

Artisan Support

For every custom order, a dedicated artist accompanies you from the first sketch to delivery. You are not buying a product — you are co-creating a masterpiece.

  • Dedicated single point of contact
  • 3D renders before production
  • Revisions until total satisfaction

The Process

From the mind to the table

Every miniature goes through a rigorous protocol, designed to guarantee flawless quality at every stage.

Brief & Concept

You share your vision: references, descriptions, vibe. Our team analyzes every detail to understand the soul of your character.

💡
✏️

Digital Sculpting

Our sculptors work in high resolution on the 3D model, iterating on the pose, proportions, and every narrative detail.

Client Validation

You receive a photorealistic 3D render for approval. We adjust until the vision is perfect — at no extra cost.

👁️
⚙️

Optimization & Supports

The file is carefully prepared: optimized supports, calculated orientation to minimize artifacts, layer settings adapted to the level of detail.

Quality Control

Every piece is inspected under a magnifying glass, sanded, cleaned, and photographed. Only a perfect miniature leaves our workshops.

🔍
📦

Packaging & Delivery

Custom artisanal packaging, protective foam, certificate of authenticity. Your miniature arrives like a work of art.

Our Materials

Materials chosen
for excellence

The quality of a miniature begins long before printing. We select each material for its aesthetic performance and durability.

💎

Collector Resin

High-performance resin, low viscosity, ideal for sub-millimeter details. Smooth surface, homogeneous curing, neutral pearl gray color.

Standard
⚗️

Premium ABS Resin

Reinforced formula for articulated pieces or fine elements (blades, wands, antennas). Superior impact resistance, silky matte finish.

High Resistance
🏆

Translucent Resin

For magic effects, crystals, flames, and auras. Captures light with a depth comparable to blown glass. Unique special effects.
